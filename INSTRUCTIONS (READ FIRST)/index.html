Instructions

  1. Go to Codecademy and select the course "HTML+CSS" to start learning how to build a webpage. You do not have to complete the whole course (unless you want to!) - learning the HTML basics and a bit of CSS is enough.
  2. Go to GitHub and set up your GitHub account. Choose the free plan (unless you want to pay!). If you are experiencing difficulties, feel free to find me :)
  3. Remember to make GitHub send a confirmation email to your email account and confirm your email account by following the steps shown on the email. This step is vital because if you do not confirm your email on GitHub, the whole webpage thing won't work.
  4. Name your repository/webpage. To make it a functioning webpage, there is only one way to name your repository. Seek me for help (unless you have figured it out or seen the instructions from GitHub).
  5. Download GitHub for Mac/Windows and clone the webpage/repository to your computer. If you are struggling to do this, feel free to find me.

  6. To start creating your webpage, you will need Sublime Text 2. Click on the link to go to the webpage, and download Sublime Text 2 for Mac/Windows. If you are experiencing difficulties downloading, feel free to find me.

  7. Go through the folders "choice_1" and "choice_2". When you have chosen, copy all the documents in that folder and paste it onto your webpage.
